  1. AURZA STANDARD SIZES from UK 8-20. Thanks to our 5% stretch and clever 3D pattern development we think that this will be a great solution for the majority of women.   2. AURZA MIX AND MATCH tops and skirts for those that want a separates look or have very different top and bottom body shapes
  3. AURZA CUSTOM SIZING provide your measurements (with our guidance) and we will make you a unique size dress

  3. I forgot to add international shipping, what do I do? Don't worry, we'll be in touch at the end of the campaign to sort it out.
  4. When will my PERK be delivered? We will send out gift vouchers immediately at the end of the campaign so that they are with you in time for Xmas. All the dresses, skirts and tops will be delivered by the end of January.

Finally let us know your height and we will do the rest.

You can then pay with PAYPAL or a credit card, ensuring you give us the correct shipping address.Once approved you will get an automatic email from Indiegogo confirming your purchase. We will update you on our progress throughout the campaign and at the end of the campaign we will send you a code and link to the design builder where you can create your dress and order your design on line.
