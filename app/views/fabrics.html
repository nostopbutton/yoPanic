CREPE

Our flattering viscose crepe fabric has been carefully selected because of its stretch, drape and softness. Mixed with polyester and elastane for added strength and stretch recovery it is less likely to crease than cotton. The inherent stretch of the twisted yarns gives superior comfort to the wearer as well as providing the firmness of a woven fabric that simple jersey cannot offer.

SILK

Our gorgeous Chinese silk offers the height in luxury and elegance. Known for its high quality it has a velvety touch and a peach-skin appearance. Silk’s natural properties make it comfortable to wear at any time of the year whilst its aesthetic properties make it drape and handle beautifully. The luxury of silk next to the skin is one of life’s pleasures.

LEATHER

We have selected the highest quality Italian lambskin leather for our fabric belts. It is characterized by its extreme softness and flexibility and comes in a wide colour palette to complement our core fabric range.

Faux LEATHER

Our stretch faux leather provides a more maintainable and affordable option to real leather. Soft and supple it adds a touch of sass to any outfit whether as trim or as a whole garment.
